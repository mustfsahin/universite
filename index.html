Our Vision and Mission

Sivas University of Science and Technology is a research-oriented, innovative, and entrepreneurial educational institution aiming to contribute to the country's scientific and technological development.

  • To offer an applied and project-based education model based on current technologies.
  • To support regional and national development by establishing strong collaborations with industry.
  • To provide students with the best learning environment through qualified academic staff and modern infrastructure.
